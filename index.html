Skateboarding is hardly comparable to conventional sports. There is a lack of leagues or associations that divide the sport into local, national or international hierarchies. And still organizations take care of the existence of a worldwide network for the implementation of loose competitions like the "Mastership", a kind of world championship, the X Gamesin California or regular contests. There is the "World Cup of Skateboarding" and in Germany the "Club of Skaters". In the context of these contests there are actually ranking lists of international skateboarders. Many skaters see themselves as skating rather as an individualized expression of life - sometimes as a kind of art - than as sport.

The conflict and criminalization experience that often arises from the use and "appropriation" of public space, the first origins of skating in the "dropout mentality of surfers" and the creativity demanded to date have rooted skating subculturally . So there is a certain connection between fashion, visual arts such as graffiti ,Graphic design and photography or various music scenes. For example, skateboarding culture-related varieties of punk and hardcore music are sometimes referred to as skate punk .
